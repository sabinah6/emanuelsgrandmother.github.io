Mrs. Francis Morrison (Emanuel's Grandmother)

  • Born in the 1920's
  • Played violin, came in second
  • She was very kind, caring, and assertive
  • She hated her father-in-law for reasons
  • She worked at a factory that made pills for pharmacies

His grandmother loved apple pie.

During WW2, she worked in the Pentagon. Two soldiers came up to her. She said to them, “MY HUSBAND IS OUT THERE CARRYING SUPPLIES TWICE HIS WEIGHT, WHILE YOU MOTHER******* ARE SITTING HERE LOOKING PRETTY!”

She was a strong woman.

She never remarried.
